Featured Recipe: Delicious Pizza!

Ever desire the most delicate and delicious pizza ever created by your own hands. Then you've come to the recipe. In following this recipe you will be astounded to find the overwhelming power that this pizza can bestows open the user. Your taste buds will be overwhelmed with delicious flavors of cheese, sauce, and dough! Follow the recipe to the T, and you will not be dissapointed.

  1. Prep: 20 Minutes
  2. Bake:25-30 Minutes

Ingredients Needed:

Make sure all required indgredients are acquired before you begin cooking. These items include:

  • 1 (1/4 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of sugar
  • 1-1/4 cup of warm water
  • 1/4 cup of canola oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3-1/2 to 4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 jar of homemade tomato sauce(can be purchased at italiansmakegoodsauce.com)
  • 3 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 3 cups of cheese acquired by the cow gods

Instructions:

Follow the below instructions to have a guaranteed delicious and desirable pizza!

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in water, let stand for approximetely 5 minutes. Add the tender oil and salt. Stir ferociously in the flour, approximetely a cup at a time until the dough forms into a soft tender like substence.
  2. After acquiring the perfect soft dough needed for the creation, we turn it on its backside on a floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic. Usually takes 2 to 3 minutes. Cover and let rise in a warm setting until no longer pink.
  3. Pummel the dough down with all your anger. After sacrificing it to the gods divide the dough in half. Press each half onto a greased 16-in pizza pan.
  4. Here is where we add the delicate sauce of the gods. Add the sauce, basil, and oregano for the perfect combination of flavor. Sprinkle in a manner that should not be seen the cheese that has been provided by the cow gods themselves. Finally we add the most important peice, the pepperoni is added onto the pizza to formalize its creation.
  5. Bake at 400* for approximetely 25 to 30 minutes, or until desired crispiness has been achieved.

Your amazing creation has been achieved and is now ready for consumption. Enjoy, you've earned it!
